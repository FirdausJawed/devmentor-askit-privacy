Privacy Policy for DevMentor AskIt

Last Updated: May 2024

1. Introduction

DevMentor AskIt ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

When you use DevMentor AskIt, we collect:

  • Query Content: The programming questions and code snippets you submit through the extension
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data with the extension to improve functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing your queries through our AI model to provide programming assistance
  • Improving the extension's functionality and user experience
  • Maintaining and optimizing the service

4. Data Sharing and Transfer

Your queries are processed using the Hugging Face API. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Any data sharing is strictly limited to what is necessary for providing the service.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your queries are transmitted securely, and we do not store any personal data permanently.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of future data collection

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
